Monthly Goals

Temple Baptisms

⛪ Goal: Once per month · Coordinated through invitation from the Bishop

Each quorum should plan at least one temple baptism per month. Coordinate with the bishop for scheduling and ensure youth bring family names when possible.

Planning Checklist

Before finalizing any activity

☐ Does this strengthen testimonies?
☐ Will most youth enjoy it?
☐ Are there school or sports conflicts?
☐ Is it within budget?
☐ Has transportation been planned?
☐ Are youth involved in planning?
☐ Does it build unity?
☐ Is there a backup plan?

Common schedule conflicts to watch for

🏈 Sports
  • Football
  • Basketball
  • Volleyball
  • Community sports
🎭 School
  • School plays
  • Band concerts
  • Choir concerts
  • Club activities
🎄 Holidays
  • Thanksgiving
  • Christmas break
  • Spring break
  • Summer vacations
⚠ Schedule may shift for Stake Conference, fundraisers, temple open house, youth camps and FSY, Halloween, Christmas party, or other major ward events. Adjust as needed when those events fall on mutual nights.
